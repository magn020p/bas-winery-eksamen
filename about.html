ABOUT BAS WINERY

Welcome to BAS Winery, where passion meets craftsmanship! Nestled in the heart of Møn, Denmark’s enchanting wine area, our story begins with two dreamers: Jørgen, a curious and innovative winemaker, and his wife, Beatriz, a savvy operations director with a long experience from big cooperate working with Business Administration.

Try our wines at BAS Winery and experience the alchemy of blending. Taste the fusion of grapes and local fruits, and raise your glass to the artistry that flows from vine to bottle. Cheers to curiosity, creativity, and the magic of terroir!

Meet Jørgen, a globe-trotting wine enthusiast with a penchant for adventure. Born in the heart of Southern Jutland, Jørgen's life has been a symphony of flavors, cultures, and curiosity. His passport boasts stamps from vineyards in Tuscany, Napa Valley, and even the sun-kissed slopes of Argentina. But it was in the unlikeliest of places that he found his true calling: In the ocean view soil of Denmark.
